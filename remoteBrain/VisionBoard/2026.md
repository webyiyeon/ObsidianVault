# 🎓 2026 Goals, Visions, Plans 
##### let each year get sweeter and sweeter <3

---
#### ✨ This Year, I Want To... 
 
1. Exercise around three times a week to maintain my physical health.
2. Take at least one post-meal walk each day, even if it’s short.
3. Spend a minimum of 10 minutes tidying up every day.
4. Eat my meals on time and log them honestly.
5. Engage with Japanese at least once a week, even in a light or casual way.
6. Save at least KRW 600,000 every month without postponing it.
7. Stay on top of my graduate classes and assignments throughout the year.
8. Write on my blog consistently, aiming for one post each month and staying active weekly.
9. Read regularly, even if it’s just for five minutes at a time.
10. Switch to recovery mode when my energy is low and allow myself to rest.
11. Study drawing at least once a week to keep improving my visual and creative skills.
